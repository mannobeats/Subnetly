# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Subnetly respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report incidents to project maintainers via GitHub issues/discussions or a private security advisory when appropriate.
